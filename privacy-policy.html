Privacy Policy

AppAmbit Inc.

Effective Date: August 25, 2025

At AppAmbit Inc. (“AppAmbit,” “we,” “our,” or “us”), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our websites, SDKs, and hosted services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and credentials you provide.
  • Usage Data: Logs, events, crashes, and telemetry submitted via the SDK.
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, IP address, browser, and connection details.
  • Payment Data: If you subscribe, billing information is collected by our payment providers (we do not store payment card details).

2. How We Use Information

We use data to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve AppAmbit services.
  • Authenticate accounts and secure access.
  • Communicate service updates and product notifications.
  • Analyze usage to improve reliability and performance.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Authorities when required by law or legal process.
  • Business Transfers in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Retention

  • We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.
  • Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting hello@appambit.com.

5. Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. However, no system is completely secure. Users should protect their login credentials.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • AppAmbit is not directed at children under 13.
  • If you are under the age of majority in your jurisdiction, you may only use AppAmbit with parental or guardian consent.
  • If we become aware of data collected from a child without consent, we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • File a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise your rights, contact hello@appambit.com.

8. International Users

If you access AppAmbit from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where we operate.

9. Third-Party Links

AppAmbit may link to external websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated via email or posted on our site.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at hello@appambit.com.
